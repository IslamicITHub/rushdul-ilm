### 🎓 Concept: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

**What it is:**
RAG is a technique that gives an AI model "seasonal memory." Standard AI models (like Qwen) only know what they were trained on. RAG allows the model to look up specific information from our database (Qdrant) *before* answering a question. This ensures the answer is based on approved Islamic sources, not the model's imagination.

**Analogy:**
Imagine an AI is a student taking an open-book exam. 
- **Standard AI:** The student tries to answer from memory.
- **RAG:** The student first looks up the topic in a trusted textbook (our Islamic database) and then writes an answer based *only* on what they read in that book.

**LlamaIndex** is the framework (the "librarian") that helps the student find the right page in the book quickly.
